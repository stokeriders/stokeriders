IKO Certifcations

Our IKO Instructors can certify you, issue you an IKO card and a streamer to enable you to kiteboard in Chicago. Montrose beach is the only beach in Chicago where kitesurfing is allowed. A completed card will show you have the skills to access highly regulated kite boarding beaches around the world and allow you to rent kiteboarding gear in select locations worldwide.
